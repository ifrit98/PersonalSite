A Bayesian-factorized approach to raw-audio transcription, composition, and synthesis, with transformer-based music generation and style-transfer evaluated both quantitatively and perceptually. The model treats musical style as a factorizable prior over generation, enabling controlled manipulation of style dimensions while preserving structural coherence.
