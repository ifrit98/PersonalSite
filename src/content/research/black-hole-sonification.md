An auditory-display pipeline that sonifies CCRG merger simulations through velocity-modulated synthesis and HRTF spatialization, turning astrophysical dynamics into perceivable sonic form. The work demonstrates that auditory display can make multi-dimensional dynamics perceivable in ways that visual display cannot easily replicate. Published in MSV '18, pp. 3–9.
