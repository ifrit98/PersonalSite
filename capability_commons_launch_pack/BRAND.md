# Brand and naming

## Project name

**Capability Commons** is the name of the public initiative founded and stewarded by Jason St George.

**Agentic Data Lite** is the name of the technical retrieval and revision substrate that powers the project.

## Allowed use

You may use the project names in descriptive and nominative ways, such as:

- saying that your work is based on or compatible with the project
- linking to the project
- discussing the project in articles, talks, or documentation

## Not allowed without permission

You may not present your fork, deployment, derivative, organization, or publication as the official Capability Commons or official Agentic Data Lite project without written permission.

This includes:

- using official logos as if your version were canonical
- presenting a fork as the primary project site
- implying endorsement by Jason St George or the official maintainers

## Official attribution

Where practical, use this attribution:

“Capability Commons is a project founded and stewarded by Jason St George.”

## Logos and visual identity

Any official logos, marks, or branded visual assets remain part of the official project identity unless otherwise stated.

## Questions

For brand and naming questions, contact the project maintainer through the official site.
