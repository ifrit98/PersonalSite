## Your Data, Your Control

Jake — a Compass broker who's been testing TurnkeyHQ — told us the two things agents actually worry about: _"Data security. Even you telling me I had to give AI the keys to my email was a bit scary. And accuracy of info being pushed by AI from me."_ Here's how we answer both.

### Data Security — Your Keys Stay Yours

- **Per-tenant encryption.** Your API keys, credentials, and OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest with AES-256 in a dedicated per-tenant secrets table. Nothing is shared with other tenants.
- **Per-tenant container isolation.** Your AI stack runs in dedicated Docker containers with their own ports and volumes — not a shared tenant pool. The curated skill library is mounted read-only; no tenant can modify skills that another tenant sees, and no tenant's workspace is reachable from another tenant's runtime. Full architecture: [`docs/security/SKILL_ISOLATION.md`](../security/SKILL_ISOLATION.md).
- **Row Level Security on every database table.** Forced at the Postgres level, validated in CI. Your contacts, transcripts, and documents are unreachable from another tenant even if an application bug tried.
- **Revoke any integration at any time.** OAuth access, API keys, and per-integration credentials can be removed in the Settings → Secrets panel with immediate effect.
- **We never train models on your data.** Vendor zero-retention flags are set on every outbound LLM call. Your client data is not fine-tuning anyone's model.
- **PII encryption for sensitive columns.** Contact PII (email, phone, full name) and call/meeting transcripts are encrypted at rest with dual-write migration already shipped.
- **Fail-closed webhook signatures.** Every inbound webhook (Retell, Twilio, HubSpot, DocuSign, Fathom) verifies HMAC signatures; missing secrets mean no acceptance, not acceptance with a warning.
- **Audit logging on every sensitive table.** Structured audit logs with DB triggers, 365-day retention, and Supabase Auth log drain. You can see exactly what the AI did on your behalf.
- **Acquisition-ready security posture.** 26-week security hardening program (Phases 0-3 complete) plus a documented 12-24 month forward roadmap covering SOC 2 Type I/II, pen test, ISO 27001, cyber insurance, and bug bounty.

### Accuracy — Approval Gates Where They Matter

- **Approval gates on every outbound communication.** Policy is defined in **`approval-policy.json`**; holds and releases are tracked in **`approval_records`**. When a send must wait for a human, the API responds with **HTTP 428** until approved. The Follow-Up Captain drafts; you approve. Voice agents follow scripts you configure. Transaction pipeline generates disclosures; you review before sending.
- **Model routing.** Primary: **DeepSeek V4** — **Flash** for standard throughput, **Pro** for heavier reasoning — with automatic tier-style selection plus **thinking mode** for premium-tier queries when extra deliberation buys accuracy. Fallback: **OpenAI** (**gpt-4.1-mini** for lighter jobs, **gpt-4.1** when frontier quality is warranted). Choices and spend roll up into **`trace_events`** for auditing.
- **Human-in-the-loop review for CMAs.** Every adjustment is editable in the CMA review UI before the branded PDF ships.
- **Structured entity extraction, not free-form summarization.** Conversation CRM extracts typed entities (contacts, properties, dates, amounts) with schema validation — so hallucinated numbers never land in your CRM.
- **Transparent action logs.** Every action the AI takes is logged with its inputs, prompts, model choice, and output — auditable in your admin console.
- **Tenant-specific knowledge.** Your business profile, ICP, preferred partners, and goals are captured in the Knowledge workspace and injected as context — so the AI acts on _your_ business logic, not a generic script.

You stay in control. The AI does the work.
